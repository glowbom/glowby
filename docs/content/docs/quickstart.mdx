## Glowbom Web

### Step 1. Open the canvas

Go to [glowbom.com](https://glowbom.com) and press **Draw**.

![Press Draw to open the Magic Window](/quickstart/step-1-draw.png)

This opens the Magic Window.

![The Magic Window canvas](/quickstart/step-1-canvas.png)

### Step 2. Draw and describe

Doodle whatever you want to build on the canvas. It does not need to look good. Add a prompt in the "Describe your creation" field if you want to give more detail.

![A rough sketch on the canvas](/quickstart/step-2-draw.png)

### Step 3. Build

Press **Build**. Glowby starts generating your prototype. You can see the progress as it works.

![Glowby generating a prototype from the drawing](/quickstart/step-2-prompt.jpg)

### Step 4. Try the prototype

When it is done, you get a working prototype you can play with right in the browser. Glowby generates the images, the code, and the whole idea for you.

![A working prototype generated from a rough sketch](/quickstart/step-3-prototype.jpg)

### Step 5. Translate to code

Translate your prototype into code for iOS (SwiftUI), Android (Jetpack Compose), or web (Next.js).

![Translate your prototype to SwiftUI, Jetpack Compose, or Next.js](/quickstart/step-4-translate.jpg)

### Step 6. Make it production ready

Use [Glowby OSS](./glowby-oss) to take your Glowbom project and make it production ready with AI coding agents, all running on your machine.

![Glowby OSS loaded with a project](/quickstart/step-5-glowbyoss.jpg)

See the [Glowby OSS guide](./glowby-oss) for setup instructions and full details.
